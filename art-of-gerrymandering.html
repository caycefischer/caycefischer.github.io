The Art of the Gerrymander

Graphic Design

Data Visualization

3D-Projection Mapping

The Art of the Gerrymander was a satirical news piece I worked on for VICE News Tonight. Alongside writer/correspondent James Hamilton and producer Izzie Mendez, we highlighted the ridiculous, unreasonable shapes congressional districts have been forced into by the process known as Gerrymandering.

I designed all the motion graphics and digital paintings for the piece, and created and revised projection graphics in real-time during the shoot in our Brooklyn studio.
