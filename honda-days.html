Born from an inside joke taken too far, The Honda Days Spectacular is an annual comedy and music variety show produced and hosted by myself, Dalton Deschain, and Giovanni Colantonio.

For five years we've transported audiences to a universe where the only winter holiday anyone has ever celebrated is the Happy Honda Days Winter Sales Event. With the help of music, magic, and 100% real surprise celebrity guests, we ape the pop culture and tech fads of the year while establishing a rich lore that has been described by fans as "needlessly complicated."
